alsegno (al-SEHN-ya)
Lightweight, self-hosted A/B review for audio & video

Share and compare revisions, and pin feedback to the exact moment — built for mixing, mastering, and post.

al segno — “to the sign.”
In a score it's the cue to jump to the marker. Here, every note pins to its moment on the timeline.

Free & open source — MIT.
